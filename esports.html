Overwatch League



What is OWL?

Overwatch League is the official Esports organization for Overwatch. It involves 20 teams based off cities much like traditional sports teams. The teams are spread out all around the world in countries such as the US, Canada, China, Korea, the UK, and France. The teams are owned by massive esports organizations such as Envyus, big corporations such as Comcast, and owners of traditional sports teams such as the New England Patriots and the NY Mets.
